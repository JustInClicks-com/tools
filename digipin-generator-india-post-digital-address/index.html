Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is DigiPIN?
DigiPIN is a standardized digital addressing system developed by India Post, providing a unique alphanumeric code for every location in India, making addresses easier to locate digitally.
How is DigiPIN different from the traditional postal PIN code?
Unlike postal PIN codes which cover large areas, DigiPIN encodes precise latitude and longitude grids to pinpoint exact locations digitally.
Can I use DigiPIN for navigation or deliveries?
Yes, DigiPIN aims to improve navigation, emergency services, and delivery accuracy by providing a precise digital address.
Is my location data stored when I generate a DigiPIN?
No, this tool does not store or share any personal location data. All calculations are done locally on your device.
Does DigiPIN work offline?
DigiPIN encoding and decoding work offline, but map and search features require an internet connection.
